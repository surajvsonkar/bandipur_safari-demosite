About Bandipur

Experience the Majesty of Bandipur Safari Lodge

Prepare to be pleasantly surprised. The majestic Nilgiris, or 'Blue Hills,' sprawled across three States of the South of India, which form part of the Western Ghats, offer a whole new experience—not to be missed by the true lover of Mother Nature.

You will notice that Bandipur is one of India's most beautiful and better-managed national parks.

Located on the Mysore-Ooty highway in Karnataka, it is spread across an area of about 874 sq km.
